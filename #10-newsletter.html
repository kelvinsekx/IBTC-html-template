## THE WEEK IN REVIEW Another tech bubble, most popular as dot-com bubble, right in our eyes in 2022., [the stocks are down, crypto is down, NFT sales too and VCs funds are dropping too](https://twitter.com/nbashaw/status/1522666477295341569?t=MI1WGJErJoq1KXgi87WKuQ&s=19) OMG. Since the cause of this one is [free money isn't free again](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/tech-layoffs-hiring-freezes-increase-cheap-money-shrinks-175749210.html), may be we can call it tech cracker instead of the bubble. For the past 5 years, tech jobs have been on the increase with even the crazier part accompanied with multiple increase in salary. Well the tech ecosystem is becoming saturated and there seem to be a tougher time ahead of us. The hard part is getting the first tech job might be even difficult as it has been. Since Rose survived the first dot-com bubble, you should take [her advice](https://twitter.com/rose_w/status/1524004922097733632?t=LsmxZkHTtx8DnC4QabeDJw&s=19) seriously. As a start up or business owner who would be seeking fund this time, [David Sacks forecast and advice](https://twitter.com/DavidSacks/status/1522745450347958272?t=HonLJsIMXejIUWCAoydR0w&s=19) would be something you would want to look into. ## STUFF THAT ARE WORTH YOUR READ ## CLUB UPDATE Last meetup number was heavily impaired by the students protest and we are putting plans on how to control such behaviour in the future, if possible we could put in plans to change venue going forward.
