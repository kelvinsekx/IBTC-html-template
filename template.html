Now that we are getting more reach and exposure, I feel we all need to learn more about being part of a community.

Our aim with IBTC is to be that community that sticks and continually achieve on it's aim. You can learn more about us here.

However when community start to grow like this, people become even more important.

In as much as more than 70% are joining this community in order to make tech connections and network, you need to know connections and trust are not built in a day.

To reap every good stuff init for this community, you need to be the person you wanted to connect with.

Here are some tips around the web to help you be a better member to a community.

  • How to ask the right questions This will expose you to how to better ask questions in tech gatherings in such a way that will excite people to help you.
  • Don't be a help vampire Communities are there to help to you, just like you too are around to help another person. Don't be an help vampire.

Just as such that you've taken the bold step to want to be a blessing to everyone else, You must be rest assured that this community would change your life vice versa.

Community isn't new and it has been a big difference to some reputable devs career

For example,
